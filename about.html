About Starr Docks
& Lifts

Starr Docks and Lifts have been manufacturing quality boat lifts and docks in Michigan for over 27 years. Whether your needs are simple, or your wants are extravagant, our superior product line will suit your needs. A waterfront lifestyle can mean different things to different people. For some it means watching the sunrise with your morning coffee, fishing with children or grandchildren, laughing loudly while splashing around in the sunshine spending time with your friends and family. Our seemingly endless configurations and many accessories that complement our docks and lifts can provide you with endless waterfront fun. Our products have been time tested and proven to provide years of worry free lakefront enjoyment. Our designs are simple for one reason just like our motto…It Just Works
